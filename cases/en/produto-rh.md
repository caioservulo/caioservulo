### ðŸ” The Challenge
*Develop and validate a new HR product for the Yampa ecosystem while minimizing the workload on the engineering team, which was focused on other strategic demands.*
> "The goal was to test a new business vertical quickly, reducing technical opportunity costs while validating market hypotheses."

---

### ðŸ”§ Strategy and Solution
*How I validated a new ecosystem front independently:*

1. **Strategic Benchmarking:** Conducted a comprehensive mapping of competitors to analyze existing solutions and identify strategic differentiators for our product ecosystem.
2. **Lean Inception:** Facilitated a Lean Inception workshop with cross-functional teams to align on the initial product scope and testing vision.
3. **Product Design & Requirements:** Mapped core features and user flows, drafting detailed requirements and acceptance criteria.
4. **Low-code Development:** Built the platform within the defined scope using low-code tools integrated with cloud databases, operating independently from the engineering team.
5. **Stakeholder Alignment:** Presented the functional scope to the CEO and Directors of Product, Marketing, and Sales to align on the first wave of tests.

---

### ðŸ“ˆ Results and Impact
*Rapid validation and operational efficiency.*

- **Speed to Market:** Developed a testable product in a reduced scope in just **9 days**.
- **Engineering Efficiency:** **Zero impact** on the engineering team's capacity during the validation phase.
- **Strategic Approval:** 100% internal approval of the solution by business units and positive initial feedback from clients.

---

### ðŸ’¡ Lessons Learned
*Insights on prototyping and product strategy:*

- **Low-code prototyping** is highly effective for validating products and market hypotheses before committing heavy engineering resources.
- Success relied heavily on effective **corporate communication** regarding the product proposal, value chain, and market fit to align stakeholders.
